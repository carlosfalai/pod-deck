Features

Studio-grade. Zero hardware.

Everything the RODECaster Pro does — minus the $700 price tag.

🎛️

32 Sound Pads (4 Banks × 8)

Load any audio file onto any pad. Four banks (A/B/C/D) give you 32 pads total. One-shot, toggle, hold, or loop — pick the trigger mode that fits your workflow.

🎚️

5 Channel Strips + Master

Mic 1, Mic 2, USB, Bluetooth, and SFXR channels — each with a vertical fader, mute, and VU meter. Full mix control, just like a real console.

⚡

Aphex Processing

Noise gate, compressor, high-pass filter, and aural exciter on every channel. Toggle each processor on/off per-strip. Broadcast-quality sound out of the box.

⏺

Multitrack Recording

Record your full mix to WAV or MP3. Arm individual tracks to capture each source separately. Built-in timer so you always know your runtime.

📞

Mix-Minus for Phone Callers

Bluetooth/phone channel sends your caller a mix that excludes their own voice — no echo, no feedback. The same pro routing the RODECaster uses, built right in.

🎨

Coloured Pads + Custom Labels

Right-click any pad to assign a colour, label, icon, and audio file. 9 colour options. Your soundboard, your brand. Looks great on stream.

Setup

Three steps. Under five minutes.

No drivers, no hardware, no complicated setup. Download and start podcasting.

01

Download & Launch

Download PodDeck free. Double-click to open. No installation wizard, no admin rights, no drivers. Works on Windows 10 and 11 out of the box.

02

Load Your Sounds

Right-click any of the 32 pads to assign an audio file. Drag in jingles, stingers, drops, and sound effects. Label and colour each one to find it fast mid-show.

03

Hit Record

Set your levels on the channel strips, enable Aphex processing, connect your phone caller to the mix-minus channel, and press record. You're live.
