**Podcast like a pro. Skip the $700 hardware.**

The RODE RODECaster Pro II is the gold standard for podcast production. PodDeck brings the same workflow to your Windows PC — without the hardware, without the price tag.

---

### What you get with Pro:

**32 Sound Pads (4 Banks × 8)**
Load jingles, stingers, sound effects, and drops onto 32 pads across 4 banks (A/B/C/D). Each pad supports One-Shot, Toggle, Hold, and Loop trigger modes. Right-click to assign a colour, label, and icon — so you find the right pad fast when you're live.

**5 Channel Strips + Master**
Mic 1, Mic 2, USB Audio, Bluetooth/Phone, and SFX — each with a vertical fader, mute button, and live VU meter. Full mix control, exactly like a real broadcast console.

**Aphex Processing Per Channel**
The RODECaster's secret weapon is its built-in Aphex processing. PodDeck includes the same chain on every channel: noise gate, compressor, high-pass filter, and aural exciter. Toggle each processor on/off per strip. Your mics will sound broadcast-ready without touching a DAW.

**EQ Presets**
Switch between Broadcast, Podcast, Music, and Raw EQ profiles with one click per channel. No guessing at frequencies.

**Mix-Minus Routing**
When you have a phone caller on air, they need to hear your mix — but NOT their own voice back. PodDeck's mix-minus routing handles this automatically. Your caller gets an echo-free feed. Same pro routing the RODECaster uses.

**Multitrack Recording**
Record your full mix to WAV or MP3, and arm individual tracks to capture each source as a separate stem. Edit each mic in post, or just keep the stereo mix. Built-in timer so you always know your runtime.

**Phantom Power Toggle**
Enable 48V phantom power per channel for condenser mics — no external preamp needed.
